About Sri Vasavi Institute of Engineering and Technology

SVIET was established in 2007, affiliated with JNTUK, and approved by AICTE. The college is NAAC accredited and offers high-quality technical education in a green and peaceful environment.

Vision

To be a premier rural engineering institution in the country and to produce engineers who are technically competent, socially responsible, and ethical.

Mission

  • Provide quality education through effective teaching-learning practices.
  • Promote industry interaction and research.
  • Impart ethical and value-based education.
